About Us

TOGETHER WE CAN CHANGE LIVES

WHAT WE STAND FOR

At Hayatu Charity Foundation, we believe in the power of compassion. Our mission is to provide the most vulnerable communities with access to basic needs like education, shelter, and healthcare. Together, we can help make the world a better place for everyone.

WE'RE MAKING A HUGE DIFFERENCE

Every child deserves a chance to succeed. We are focused on providing opportunities for children to thrive, whether through education, emotional support, or essential resources. We are here to lift them up, one step at a time.

OUR MISSION

We aim to provide quality care and support for children and communities in need, ensuring that no one is left behind.

OUR STORY

Founded on the principles of kindness and giving,
Hayatu Charity Foundation has been dedicated to serving those who need it most. We strive to bring about meaningful change in the lives of individuals and families, working toward a more sustainable future.

OUR OBJECTIVES

  • To provide access to quality education for underprivileged children.
  • To offer healthcare support and medical aid to vulnerable communities.
  • To ensure food security and provide shelter for those in need.
  • To empower communities through skill development and vocational training.
  • To advocate for the rights and well-being of children and marginalized groups.

Over the years,
we have seen lives transformed through the generosity of our donors and volunteers. Our foundation is built on the belief that even small contributions can create lasting impact.

We Have Helped Over 200 People

Through your generosity, we've been able to provide shelter, food, and education to over 200 individuals in need. With your support, we continue to make a lasting impact and transform lives. Together, we can do even more.
