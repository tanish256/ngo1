Contribute To Qurban

Qurban, the sacred act of animal sacrifice during Eid al-Adha, is a beautiful tradition that honors the spirit of Prophet Ibrahim’s devotion and obedience to Allah. At Hayatu Charity Foundation, we ensure your Qurban reaches families in need — those who may not enjoy meat all year. Your generous donation brings nourishment, joy, and dignity to the most vulnerable. Join us in fulfilling this spiritual obligation and making Eid truly meaningful for many.

What We Have Done So Far

Thanks to your support, our past Qurban campaigns have successfully delivered meat packages to hundreds of families in remote and underserved communities. From the careful selection of healthy animals to the respectful distribution on Eid day, every step is managed with transparency and compassion. Each smile, prayer, and shared meal reminds us why Qurban is more than a tradition — it’s a symbol of unity and love.

Through your contributions, we've been able to distribute fresh meat to orphanages, widows, the elderly, and struggling families who rarely get to enjoy such nourishment. These moments of giving go beyond food — they build bridges of kindness, restore hope, and remind us all of the true essence of Eid: sacrifice, compassion, and community.
